Cookies Policy

Ovo-Games.github.io ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies to enhance the user experience and improve our website's performance. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences regarding cookies.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and actions over time, making it easier for you to navigate and use the site. Cookies can also be used to collect information about how you interact with the site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website. They enable basic functionalities like page navigation and accessing secure areas of the site.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how users interact with our site, such as which pages are visited most often. This helps us improve the performance and user experience of our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your preferences, such as language settings or the region you are in, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display relevant ads to users based on their interests and browsing history. We may use third-party advertising services, such as Google AdSense, which may place cookies on your device for this purpose.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also allow third-party services (such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and others) to set cookies on your device. These third parties may use the data collected to track your online behavior across different websites and provide targeted advertising.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.
